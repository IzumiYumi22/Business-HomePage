About us

Welcome to LumaPhane, your premier destination for beautifully crafted framed lithophanes. Launched in 2024, LumaPhane has been a passion project many years in the making. We understand the importance of preserving your most precious moments. That's why we have meticulously perfected the process to protect and showcase your memories. LumaPhanes are brought to life by using in-house manufacturing techniques with resin-based 3D printing technology. Coupled with beautifully crafted custom wooden frames, your cherished memories will be preserved and enjoyed in a unique and stylish way. Whether you're looking to display your own special moments or searching for the perfect gift for a loved one, we have you covered.
